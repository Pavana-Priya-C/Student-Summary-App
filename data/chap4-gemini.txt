Horace Danby, a skilled locksmith, is also a thief who steals from wealthy people to buy rare books. He plans a robbery at Shotover Grange, but encounters the owner's wife, a cunning woman who tricks him into opening the safe and then accuses him of the theft. Horace, who believed in the concept of 'honour among thieves', is surprised by the woman's deception and ends up in prison as a result.
