Sulekha, a girl with a disability, is sent to school by her father, who is pressured by a government official.  Despite facing ridicule and fear, she learns to read and write, becoming a capable woman.  Later, when her family tries to arrange a marriage with a greedy man, she refuses and stands up for herself. Sulekha then decides to use her skills to teach in the same school where she learned, proving that she is not the 'simpleton' everyone thought she was.
